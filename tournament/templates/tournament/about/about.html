About Us

Established in 2018, Nepal Commerce Campus (NCC) has quickly become a prominent institution in Nepal, known for its competitive spirit and commitment to excellence. Our participation in the BIM Cricket Tournament is a testament to our dedication to fostering talent and promoting sportsmanship among our students.

Under the guidance of our experienced coach, Basanta Khatri, and the leadership of our captain, Manjeet Thapaliya, our cricket team has consistently been a strong contender in domestic tournaments. Their strategic insights and passion for the game have driven our team to showcase exceptional skills and determination on the field.

At NCC, we believe that sports play a crucial role in character building and personal development. Our cricket program is designed to provide students with a platform to hone their skills, build confidence, and learn valuable life lessons through the sport. We are committed to creating an inclusive and supportive environment where every student has the opportunity to grow and thrive.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a pivotal force in shaping Nepalese cricket, fostering an environment that not only nurtures talent but also instills the values of sportsmanship and teamwork. We aim to be a beacon of inspiration for young players, encouraging them to chase their dreams with determination and resilience. By maintaining a strong sense of brotherhood, we strive to create a supportive community that transcends the boundaries of the cricket field, promoting unity and mutual respect among all members.

We are committed to providing an unforgettable cricketing experience that goes beyond the game itself. Our goal is to empower our players to reach their full potential and to offer our fans an engaging and passionate sporting experience. Through excellence in performance and a dedication to our community, we seek to create lasting memories and a legacy that will inspire future generations of cricket enthusiasts in Nepal. We believe in the power of cricket to bring people together and to contribute positively to the nation's spirit and identity.

Our Mission

Our mission is to elevate the standard of cricket in Nepal and to serve as a role model for aspiring athletes. We are committed to inspiring young players by demonstrating the value of hard work, dedication, and the power of teamwork. We believe that these qualities are not only essential for success on the field but also for personal growth and development off the field.

Furthermore, we are dedicated to contributing to the growth of cricket across Nepal. We strive to make the sport more accessible and engaging for all, regardless of their background. By doing so, we hope to create a strong foundation for the future of cricket in Nepal, one that is built on passion, skill, and a love for the game.
